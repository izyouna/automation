# ADR Template

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

**Status:** Proposed

## Context

*What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?*
*What are the driving forces behind this decision? These forces can be business, technical, or operational.*
*What are the constraints (e.g., budget, time, skills, existing infrastructure) that influence the decision?*
*What alternatives were considered?*

## Decision

*What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?*
*Be specific and clear. This should be a statement of the chosen approach.*
*Justify the decision. Why was this option chosen over the alternatives?*

## Consequences

*What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?*
*What are the positive, negative, and neutral consequences of this decision?*
*How will this decision impact the team, the codebase, the operations, and the users?*
*Are there any new risks or trade-offs introduced by this decision?*
